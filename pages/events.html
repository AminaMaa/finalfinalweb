Memorable Events

A collection of events that shaped my journey, from workshops and hackathons to team collaborations. Each experience brought new challenges, valuable lessons, and unforgettable moments!

IEEEXtreme 18.0

Participating in IEEEXtreme 18.0 was an unforgettable experience that tested my limits and deepened my passion for coding. From the moment the 24-hour clock started, the atmosphere was electric, filled with energy and determination. Working alongside my team, we faced a series of algorithmic challenges that pushed our problem-solving skills to the edge. The sense of collaboration and camaraderie was incredible—we celebrated each solved problem like a victory and strategized fiercely for the next.

MindShift Hackathon

The Mindshift Hackathon, hosted at ENET'Com Sfax, was a thrilling event that brought together creative minds, passionate innovators, and problem solvers under one roof. This dynamic competition challenged participants to develop groundbreaking solutions to real-world problems within a limited timeframe. The event fostered a spirit of collaboration, where diverse teams combined their technical expertise, creativity, and strategic thinking to craft innovative projects.
